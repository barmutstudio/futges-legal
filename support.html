Common topics

I forgot my password

Use the “Forgot password” link on the sign-in screen to request a reset code sent to your email address.

I want to delete my account

You can deactivate or permanently delete your account from Settings → Account inside the app. You can also see the account deletion page or email barmutstudio@gmail.com.

Reporting content / users

Use the in-app report/block tools, or report to barmutstudio@gmail.com. Reported content is reviewed within 24 hours.
